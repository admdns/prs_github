Corporate Profile

Your reliable partner worldwide...

PORAS Group is a specialist in the manufacturing of nonferrous metal raw and semi products and furthermore the group manufactures precision components and assemblies for various industrial and professional use since 1978.

Our specialized workforce and technical solutions as a result of engineering R&D offer our business partners the required production facilities and services.

PORAS Group has integrated plants and warehouses for better customer service.

With quality assured and internationally approved products, we meet current and potential needs of international and national clients. All products are custom made and delivered according to EN or local regulations demanded by our clients worldwide. PORAS Group has an ISO9001:2000 standard approved system.

Today, with continuing R&D and innovations, PORAS Group maintains its capacity and quality standards in the global market. QUALITY AND TECHNICAL SERVICES are strong points of our mission.

An experienced and friendly staff is always ready to provide you with answers to all your questions.
